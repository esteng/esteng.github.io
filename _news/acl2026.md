Excited to announce three papers accepted to ACL 2026! [GrAInS](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.18043): gradient-based attribution for inference-time steering of LLMs and VLMs. [PRInTS](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.19314): reward modeling for long-horizon information seeking. [Routing with Generated Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.09692): annotation-free LLM skill estimation and expert selection.
