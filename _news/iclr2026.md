Five papers accepted to ICLR 2026! [Calibrating Verbalized Confidence with Self-Generated Distractors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.25532): improving LLM confidence calibration using self-generated distractors. [Gistify!](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26790): codebase-level understanding via runtime execution. [One Life to Learn](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.12088): inferring symbolic world models for stochastic environments from unguided exploration. [PoSh](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.19060): using scene graphs to guide LLMs-as-a-Judge for detailed image descriptions. [TrustGen](https://openreview.net/forum?id=Fcf5fLmaeG): a platform for dynamic benchmarking on the trustworthiness of generative foundation models.
