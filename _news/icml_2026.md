Three papers accepted to ICML 2026! [Multimodal Fact-Level Attribution for Verifiable Reasoning](), which extends fact-level attribution to multimodal settings to support verifiable reasoning. [Skill-Based Mixture-of-Experts: Adaptive Routing for Heterogeneous Reasoning via Inferred Skills](), which routes inputs to specialized experts by inferring the skills required for each reasoning task. [Generalized Correctness Models: Learning Calibrated and Cross-Model Correctness Predictors from Historical Patterns](), which learns correctness predictors that generalize across models by leveraging historical performance patterns.
